Maine Coon (Cat)

Maine Coon cat is the oldest and most natural domestic breed of cats which is found in Maine, North America, as the name suggests.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 1 meter
  • Average Lifespan: 13 years
  • Habitat: Domestic
